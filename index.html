Privacy Policy VidX Lab

Last updated: December 20, 2025

VidX Lab (“we”, “our”, or “us”) develops Android applications including VidX Player, VidX Music, Neu Music, Neu Video, VidX Camera, VidX Calculator, ImageLab, VidX AppLock, VidX Gallery, VidX Files & Good Habit. We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our apps handle information.

By using our apps, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users on our own servers.

Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect personal details such as your name, email, address, or contacts;
  • Upload or store your photos, videos, or other media;
  • Track your precise location or unique identifiers for profiling.

All media and content remain on your device unless you explicitly share or move them using your device’s features.

2. Third-Party Services (Firebase Analytics)

Our apps use Google Firebase Analytics to understand app performance and usage trends (e.g., screen views, feature usage, crash data). This helps us improve stability and user experience.

Firebase may process the following categories of data:

  • Device and app information (e.g., device model, OS version, app version);
  • Usage events and diagnostics (e.g., screen opens, feature taps);
  • Crash reports and performance metrics.

We configure Firebase with privacy in mind. Reports we access are aggregated and do not include personally identifiable information. Learn more at the official resources:

  • Firebase Privacy & Security
  • Google Privacy Policy

3. Permissions We Use

Our apps request only the permissions needed for core features. Examples include:

  • Storage/Media Access — to browse, edit, or delete photos/videos on your device;
  • Biometric/App Lock — to secure the app using PIN, pattern, or fingerprint;
  • Network Access — used only for analytics and crash reporting.

Permissions operate locally on your device; your content is not transmitted to us.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain user data on our servers. Analytics data handled by Firebase is retained according to Google’s policies and your device settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Choices

You can limit analytics collection by adjusting your device settings (e.g., Ads personalization, Usage & diagnostics) or within the app if an analytics toggle is provided.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at vidxplay@gmail.com.
