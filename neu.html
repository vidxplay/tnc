Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the following applications:

  • Neu Phone — Smart Dialer
  • Neu Messages — Fast SMS App

Your privacy is a core principle of our apps. They are built with a privacy-first approach. Your communication data stays on your device and under your control. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Data Collection

Both apps operate primarily using on-device data only.

  • We do NOT collect contacts to external servers
  • We do NOT upload call logs or SMS messages
  • We do NOT track message or call content
  • We do NOT build advertising profiles
  • We do NOT sell or share personal data

All call history, SMS messages, and contacts are processed locally on your device.

Permissions Usage

Neu Phone — Smart Dialer

  • Contacts — display and search contacts
  • Call Logs — show recent and missed calls
  • Phone — place and manage calls
  • Default Dialer Role — enable dialer features

Neu Messages — Fast SMS App

  • SMS — send, receive, and display messages
  • Contacts — show contact names in conversations
  • Notifications — message alerts
  • Default SMS Role — enable messaging features

Permissions are used strictly for core functionality and never for advertising or tracking.

Internet Access

Core calling and messaging features work without cloud data collection. If network access is used (such as optional updates or crash diagnostics), it does not include personal communication content.

Communication Content

  • We do not read call audio
  • We do not store SMS content externally
  • We do not transmit contact details
  • We do not upload conversation history

All such data remains stored locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

These apps are not directed to children and do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated if features change. The latest version will always be published on this page.
