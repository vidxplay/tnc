Privacy Policy — Neu Apps (Neu Phone, Neu Contacts, Neu SMS)

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the following mobile applications:

  • Neu Phone — calling and dialer application
  • Neu Contacts — contact management application
  • Neu SMS — SMS and messaging application

(collectively referred to as the “Apps”).

“We”, “us”, and “our” refer to the developer of these Apps.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our Apps.

Information We Access

Our Apps may access certain information stored on your device only to provide app functionality. The specific data types accessed depend on which App you use.

Neu Phone — Dialer & Calling App

Neu Phone may access:

  • Contacts — to display and manage your contacts
  • Call History / Call Logs — to show and manage recent calls
  • Phone & Call Status — to place and receive calls
  • Microphone — only during active phone calls
  • Dual-SIM information (where supported) — to allow SIM selection for calls

Neu Contacts — Contact Manager

Neu Contacts may access:

  • Contacts — to display, search, edit, and manage your contacts
  • Account information (optional) — to assign contacts to an account where supported

Neu Contacts does not create or store its own cloud backup.

Neu SMS — Messaging App

Neu SMS may access:

  • SMS messages — to send, receive, and display SMS
  • MMS data (if supported) — to send and receive multimedia messages
  • Contacts — to match phone numbers with contact names
  • Phone state — for messaging functionality

Android requires Neu SMS to be set as the default SMS app to send and receive SMS/MMS.

How Your Data Is Used

Across all Apps:

  • Data is accessed only to provide app functionality
  • We do not upload your personal data to our own servers
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
  • All personal data remains stored on your device or your chosen sync provider (for example, Google Contacts if enabled)

We do NOT use your data for analytics, profiling, or advertising.

No Tracking or Advertising

Our Apps:

  • do not use advertising SDKs
  • do not use third-party analytics tools
  • do not track user behavior
  • do not use cookies

Permissions

Each App requests only the minimum permissions required to function. You may revoke permissions at any time from your device settings, but features that rely on those permissions may stop working.

Security

Because our Apps do not transmit or store your personal data externally, your information remains on your device and under your control.

Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are not designed for children under 13 and do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted here with a revised “Last Updated” date.
